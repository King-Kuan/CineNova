Welcome to CineNova ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by The Palace Tech House — The Palace, Inc. 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Comments: When you post a comment on a movie, we collect your name and optionally your email address, along with the text of your comment.
  • Advertiser Applications: When you apply to advertise, we collect your business name, contact name, email, phone number, and ad content details.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect anonymised data about how you interact with the site, including pages visited, download events, and session duration, via Firebase Analytics.
  • Device Data: We may collect device type, browser type, IP address, and referral URLs for operational and security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your comments on movie pages
  • To process and review advertiser applications
  • To track download counts and improve content recommendations
  • To understand how users interact with the site (analytics)
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Third-Party Services

CineNova uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase / Firestore — database and authentication services
  • Google Analytics (via Firebase) — anonymised usage analytics
  • ImageKit — image hosting and optimisation
  • YouTube — trailer embedding (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Google Drive / MEGA / MediaFire — third-party file hosting for downloads

We do not control these third parties and are not responsible for their data practices.

5. Cookies

We use session storage and browser cookies to manage ad display frequency (e.g., showing a popup ad only once per session). We do not use cookies for cross-site tracking or advertising profiling. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may be affected.

6. Data Retention

Comments are retained indefinitely unless removed by our admin team. Advertiser application data is retained for as long as necessary to process and fulfill the business relationship. Analytics data is retained per Google Firebase's standard retention policies.

7. Children's Privacy

CineNova is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately.
