DMCA Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  CineNova by The Palace Tech House

Our Position on Copyright

CineNova respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users and advertisers to do the same. CineNova operates as an index site — we do not host, upload, or store any movie files on our servers. All download links point to third-party file hosting platforms (Google Drive, MEGA, MediaFire) which are not under our control.

⚡ Fast Takedown

We take copyright concerns seriously. Valid DMCA notices are actioned within 48–72 hours. Please use the contact form or email us directly with the required information below.

Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you are a copyright owner or authorised agent and believe that content indexed on CineNova infringes your copyright, you may submit a DMCA takedown notice. Your notice must include all of the following:

  • Your full legal name and a description of your role (copyright owner or authorised agent)
  • A description of the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed
  • The exact URL(s) on CineNova where the allegedly infringing material appears
  • Your contact information: email address, mailing address, and telephone number
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorised to act on their behalf
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Send your completed notice to us via the Contact page. Incomplete notices may not be processed.

What Happens After You File

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  • Remove or disable access to the identified content within 48–72 hours
  • Notify the user who posted the content (if applicable)
  • Document the takedown in our records

Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice. A valid counter-notice must include your name, contact details, identification of the removed content, a statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the removal was a mistake or misidentification, your consent to local court jurisdiction, and your signature.

Repeat Infringers

CineNova will, in appropriate circumstances, disable and/or terminate access for users who are repeat infringers of intellectual property rights.
